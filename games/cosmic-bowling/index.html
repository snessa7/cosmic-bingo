How to Play Cosmic Bowling

Basic Controls

• Use the Position slider to move the ball left/right

• Use the Angle slider to adjust your throw angle

• Hold the BOWL! button to charge power

• Release to throw the ball

• Press SPACEBAR to pause

Touch Controls

• Swipe left/right to position the ball

• Tap and hold to charge power

• Release to bowl

Power-ups

⚡ Super Ball: Increased power and speed

🎯 Split Ball: Ball splits into two on first pin contact

💥 Explosive Ball: Creates an explosion on impact

Scoring

• Strike (X): All pins in one roll = 10 + next 2 rolls

• Spare (/): All pins in two rolls = 10 + next 1 roll

• 10th frame: Up to 3 rolls if you get strikes/spares

Tips

• Aim for the pocket (between pins 1-3 for right-handers)

• Use angle to create pin action

• Power-ups recharge after each frame
